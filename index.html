Integrated Twitch Chat

Verification Page for Riot & Overwolf


Integrated Twitch Chat is an overlay tool that puts chat from your Twitch channel on-screen in League of Legends so that you don’t have to read another monitor to engage with your Twitch audience when streaming. This allows players to play in full-screen exclusive mode instead of windowed or borderless, while still staying connected with their viewers.

Key Features

  • It displays only chat messages from the streamer’s own Twitch channel.
  • It does not interact with, read from, or modify the game client in any way.
  • It does not provide gameplay advantages, automation, or additional data beyond what is already publicly available in Twitch chat.
  • The font style and colors are fixed and not user-customizable.
  • Players can adjust the size, position, and transparency of the chat window to ensure it does not interfere with core gameplay or UI elements.
  • The application includes no monetization features and is available entirely free to all users.

The purpose of Integrated Twitch Chat is to enhance the streaming experience and audience engagement without impacting competitive integrity or the normal operation of League of Legends.
